Atenean Pilgrim


The essence of being an Atenean Pilgrim—a journey that encompasses the pursuit of knowledge, the cultivation of spirituality, and the relentless pursuit of justice and service. Through images and reflections, we seek to capture the transformative power of the Ateneo experience and inspire you to embrace your role as a catalyst for positive change in the world.
